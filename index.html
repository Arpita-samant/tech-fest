tech fest

Tech Geeks Club, in collaboration with the School of Computing, is organizing Nirvana Tech Fest 2025 on October 17 and 18. This two-day event will bring together students from various schools and colleges to celebrate technology, creativity, and innovation. The fest will feature a wide range of activities including hackathons, workshops, seminars, science exhibitions, coding competitions, and certification examinations.
